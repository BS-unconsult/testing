Step 1 of 4

Tell us about your organisation

Eight plain-language questions about your size, governance, and current EDI practices. No trick questions — just honest answers about where you actually are.

1. How many paid staff does your organisation have?
2. Do you have a dedicated HR function or HR business partner?
3. How does your board currently engage with EDI?
4. Do you currently collect any workforce diversity data?
5. Do you have a written EDI strategy or policy?
6. Have you ever run a staff survey with questions about inclusion or belonging?
7. How would you honestly describe your organisation's current EDI culture?
8. What is your primary motivation for using this tool today?
